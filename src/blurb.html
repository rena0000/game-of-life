Conway’s Game of Life

The Game of Life is a cellular automation created by John Conway.

The Rules

Cells that are alive

  • Cells with 2-3 alive neighbours will survive
  • Cells with 4+ alive neighbours die (overpopulation)
  • Cells with 1 or less neighbours die (solitude)

Cells that are empty

  • Cells with exactly 3 alive neighbours will become alive
